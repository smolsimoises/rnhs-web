ABM stands for Accountancy, Business, and Management. It is an academic strand or track within the education system that focuses on providing students with a strong foundation in business, finance, and management principles. The ABM strand is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge for careers in fields such as accounting, entrepreneurship, marketing, business management, and related areas. Students who choose the ABM strand often have an interest in pursuing careers in business, finance, or management roles in various industries. The curriculum typically includes subjects related to accounting principles, business ethics, economics, finance, and strategic management. The ABM strand aims to prepare students for further education in business-related courses or for entry into the workforce with a solid background in accountancy, business, and management.
